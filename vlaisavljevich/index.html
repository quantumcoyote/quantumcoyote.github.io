We are computational chemists

Our focus is to provide molecular-level insight across a broad spectrum of chemical problems. From sustainability to fundamental questions in electronic structure, our team uses quantum chemical and classical methods to build models in collaboration with experimentalists. Additionally, we seek to create a collaborative and inclusive environment in our group. Current team members are from Bangladesh, Iran, Ghana, Vietnam, Mexico, India, Iran, and the US.

  • Heavy Elements

    Through high-level computational modeling including relativistic corrections, the role of the 5f and 6d orbitals can be understood.
