Vlaisavljevich Group

By taking a hierarchical approach where multiple methods are used in combination, fundamental insights can be made in complex systems: where the complexity may arise either from the number of atoms or from strong electron correlation. Our research aims to tackle diverse problems in inorganic chemistry, f-element chemistry, and materials science. Applications include the trapping of heavy elements in aqueous media, information storage/electronic devices, catalysis, and as environmental sensors.
