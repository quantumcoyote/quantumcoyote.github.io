What can you do with a Chemistry Degree from USD?

USD Chemistry will take your interest in chemistry and expand upon it in an extraordinary fashion! Through a combination of fascinating lectures from world renowned USD chemistry professors to practical, hands-on training in the best equipped laboratories, we will prepare you for whatever career in science and medicine you desire. The curriculum is approved by the American Chemical Society (ACS) for superior preparation for industry, postgraduate studies or professional careers. Students have the opportunity to work with state-of-the-art instrumentation and become involved in research projects that make a difference. Begin working with experts in the elds of battery technology, fingerprint analysis, pollution detection, nanotechnology, catalysis and more.
